Our Project

In the framework of the outsourcing law, companies that subcontract personnel have certain responsibilities. Our client's need was a tool to facilitate compliance with these obligations. We developed a multi-company platform that manages environments for suppliers, performs controls, and provides reports to clients, ensuring legal compliance.
